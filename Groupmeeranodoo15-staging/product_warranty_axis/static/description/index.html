Product Warranty Management in odoo, set warranty period in days, months, years

Product Warranty management module you can assign single or multiple warranties to your products, create forms and warranty presets, set warranty period from odoo backend, add warranty thumbnails, enable product warranty to renew and more. Manage all your requests in a single page, change request status with one click, send an email to your customers directly from the request manager pages, add an unlimited warranty to a product, easily sell warranties with your products, add warranties to your variable products, enable an easy overview of warranty requests for your customers, allow multiple warranty requests using this odoo module. User can verify Claim and extend the warranty.

Warranty module having functionaltiy to use for Call / Ticket Customer Service Management in odoo module. You can send a notification to the clients/customers to renew Warranty when your product warranty is expired. Under the contract, the product provides service to customers from complaint management, email, warranty management, call management, and product issue management.
